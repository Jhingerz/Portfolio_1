About Me

I specialize in end-to-end lifecycle automation and CRM marketing, leveraging advanced email and SMS strategies to help businesses grow and engage their customers effectively. My expertise spans across the entire customer lifecycle — from initial platform setup and integration to sophisticated campaign design, analytics, and ongoing optimization.

Email Platform Setup & Integration: I manage seamless onboarding and configuration of email marketing platforms such as Klaviyo, Mailchimp, Salesforce Marketing Cloud, and others. This includes integrating with eCommerce stores, CRMs, and other marketing tools to enable personalized, data-driven customer journeys.

Campaign Strategy & Automation: I design and execute targeted campaigns, including welcome series, abandoned cart flows, win-back campaigns, and post-purchase nurturing, using behavior-based triggers and segmentation. These automation flows deliver the right message at the right time to maximize engagement and conversions.

Template Design & Testing: Skilled in creating responsive, brand-aligned email templates using HTML, MJML, and drag-and-drop builders like BeeFree. I implement rigorous A/B and multivariate testing to optimize subject lines, content, and design for peak performance.

Analytics & Reporting: I build comprehensive dashboards and generate detailed reports tracking KPIs such as open rates, click-through rates, conversion rates, and revenue attribution. Leveraging tools like Tableau, Power BI, and Supermetrics, I provide actionable insights to continuously improve campaign outcomes.

Compliance & Deliverability: Ensuring campaigns meet GDPR, CAN-SPAM, and other regulatory standards is a priority. I handle domain/IP warm-up, monitor sender reputation, and conduct deliverability audits to maintain high inbox placement rates and minimize bounce and spam complaints.

Customer Lifecycle Focus: From acquisition to retention and re-engagement, my approach centers on delivering personalized, meaningful communication at every stage of the customer journey. By aligning strategy with customer behavior and data insights, I drive sustained growth and brand loyalty.
